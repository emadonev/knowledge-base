The distance formula is used to define the shortest distance (hypothenuse) of a right triangle connecting points $A$, $B$ and $C$.:
$$
\begin{align}
(AB)^2 = (AC)^2 + (BC)^2 \\
AB = \sqrt{(AC)^2 + (BC)^2} \\
AC = |x_2 - x_1| \\
BC = |y_2 - y_1| \\
AB = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2} 
\end{align}
$$
