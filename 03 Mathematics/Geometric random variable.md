This type of [[Distribution]] and [[Random variables]] counts the number of trials required to observe a single success! Each trial is independent and has it's own [[Probability]] of $p$. 

Function:
$$f(x;p) = (1-p)^xp $$
[[Variance]]:
$$\frac{1-p}{p^2} $$
Mean [[Statistical functions]]:
$$\frac{1}{p} $$
